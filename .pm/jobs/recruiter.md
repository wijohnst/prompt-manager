# Recruiter — Agentic Resources

## Why Now

The founder is running the interview process manually. Every hire requires the
founder to write meta-prompts, collect model outputs, score candidates, simulate
interviews, and author the base prompt. None of that requires founder authority.
Only the hire decision does. This role owns everything in between.

## What They Own

- Monitoring the job board (`.pm/jobs/`) for open roles
- Running the `agent-interview` workflow end-to-end for each open posting
- Delivering a hire recommendation and a ready-to-promote base prompt to the
  posting department head
- Maintaining interview records under `.pm/interview/<role>/`

## What They Do Not Own

- The hire decision — that stays with the department head who posted the role
- Prompt authorship beyond what the interview process produces
- Onboarding — once hired, the department head sends the welcome mail

## Done in 30 Days

First hire completed without founder involvement in the process. Founder
receives a recommendation, reviews the candidate, and approves. That is the
entire founder surface area.
